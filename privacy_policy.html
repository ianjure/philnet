Privacy Policy for PhiLNet Vanguard

PhiLNet Vanguard is a browser extension that protects you from phishing websites by sending URLs to a secure backend server for analysis.

What information we collect

  • Only the URL of the website you visit is sent to our server for phishing detection.

How we use this information

  • To determine whether the visited website is a phishing site or safe.
  • No other browsing data, personal information, or cookies are collected.

User controls

  • You can whitelist trusted domains; URLs for these sites will not be sent to our server.
  • Detection history is stored locally in your browser only.

Data security

All URLs sent for phishing detection are transmitted securely via HTTPS. We do not store any personal or sensitive user data beyond the minimal URL required for phishing detection.
